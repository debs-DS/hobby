About

Hi, my name's Debs, a teenage girl who loves being organized. My favorite thing to do when I get up in the morning is look up at the sky and go on about what I plan or want to do that day. I'm a very tidy person and I LOVE to clean my room. I have loads of things that I want to achieve or do consistently by the end of the year, so I made this blog to keep track of progress, and of course, memories. I love to write as well.
So basically, this blog is going to contain stories and me ranting about my day, the things I did, things to improve ... and the like. I hope you enjoy reading what I write!!
